## Figure policy

The reviewer-facing role of this surface is:

- make the current visual evidence easy to find
- keep figures linked to the findings and summaries that interpret them
- avoid making figures stand alone without claim boundary

For interpretation, always pair this surface with:

- [`../README.md`](../README.md)
- [`../summaries/README.md`](../summaries/README.md)
