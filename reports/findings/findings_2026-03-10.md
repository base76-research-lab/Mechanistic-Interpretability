# Findings — 2026-03-10 Runs

## 1) Context & Goal
- Probed hallucination-prone behavior under L-SAE/L-SAE+R interventions on the observability panel.
- Observed that L-SAE+R “recording” rewrites the residual stream (intervention), leading to altered decision dynamics.
- Built a read-only external observer (“Transformer Oscilloscope”) to study internal dynamics without perturbation.

## 3) Findings
- **Recon is intervention (commitment operator):** stabilizes decisions, removes abstention, but does not improve factual correctness; the residual field is rewritten.
- **Supervision needs write-back to act:** L-SAE without recon ≈ baseline; L-SAE+R shapes perturbation (halluc gap ↓, anchored gap ↑) only when reconstruction is applied.
- **Injection/prompt vectors are parameterized:** prompt-vector modes/alpha/layer/token are now explicit; observation must remain read-only for causal clarity.

## 4) Rationale for Oscilloscope
Needed a non-interfering observer; Transformer Oscilloscope uses read-only forward hooks (residual/attn/MLP/logits) to visualize trajectories (entropy, gap, PCA) without modifying the model state.
