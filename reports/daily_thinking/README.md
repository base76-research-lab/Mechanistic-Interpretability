# Daily Thinking

This directory is the internal project diary for the research process.

It exists for material that is too important to leave only in chat history, but too provisional or
process-oriented to present as a formal findings note.

Use it for:

- daily reasoning summaries
- turning points in interpretation
- why a method or framing changed
- open tensions between competing explanations
- informal but durable links between runs, ideas, and next steps

It should not replace:

- `findings/` for evidence-bearing result notes
- `syntheses/` for higher-level research conclusions
- `internal_ops/` for week plans and review packs

## Suggested structure for each day

- what was run
- what changed in understanding
- what hypotheses strengthened
- what claim boundaries tightened
- what remains unresolved
- what the day handed forward
